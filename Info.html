Connected Textures

If you have Connected Clear Glass installed, you will need OptiFine for the Connected Textures to work.

if you have OptiFine installed go to Options Video Settings Quality Connected Textures = Fancy.


Known Bugs

  • Bug Connected Clear Glass and Clear Glass [x32] show broken textures.
    Fix Use OptiFine.
    Caused by Minecraft.

    Bug OptiFine breaks Clear Glass [x16].
    Fix Switch Connected Textures to OFF.
    Caused by OptiFine.
