Connected Textures

If you have Connected Clear Glass installed, you will need OptiFine for the Connected Textures to work.

if you have OptiFine installed go to Options ➤ Video Settings ➤ Quality ➤ Connected Textures = Fancy.

Known Bugs

MC 1.0 - 1.4:

  • Connected Clear Glass and Clear Glass [x32] has some broken textures (can be fixed by using OptiFine). [Game Bug]
  • OptiFine breaks Clear Glass [x16] (can be fixed by switching Connected Textures to OFF). [OptiFine Bug]
